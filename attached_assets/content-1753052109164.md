## Apple Pencil Pro

[Buy](https://www.apple.com/us/shop/goto/product/MX2D3)

Apple Pencil Pro adds even more magical capabilities to help bring your ideas to life. Advanced features like squeeze, barrel roll, and haptic feedback make marking up, taking notes, and creating a masterpiece more intuitive than ever. Use Apple Pencil hover to preview exactly where Apple Pencil Pro will touch down on the display,[1](https://www.apple.com/apple-pencil/#footnote-1) and double-tap to quickly switch tools. And if you misplace it while you’re out and about or at home, you can easily locate it in the Find My app. Apple Pencil Pro pairs, charges, and stores magnetically on the side of your iPad.

- ### Squeeze



Apple Pencil Pro can sense when you squeeze it and brings up a palette, so you can quickly switch tools, line weights, and colors.









![A user's hand holds Apple Pencil Pro in a writing position. The tip is pointed towards an interface showing a new tool palette.](https://www.apple.com/v/apple-pencil/af/images/overview/pro/gallery/squeeze_startframe__dteisaesb5yu_large.jpg)

- ### Barrel roll



A gyroscope allows you to rotate Apple Pencil Pro for precise control of shaped pen and brush tools.









![A user holds Apple Pencil Pro in a writing position. The tip is pressing down on a display showing thick strokes being drawn in bright purple.](https://www.apple.com/v/apple-pencil/af/images/overview/pro/gallery/barrel-roll_startframe__b8n2wfcjlf6u_large.jpg)

- ### Haptic feedback



A custom haptic engine provides precise feedback that you can feel. When you squeeze or double-tap, you’ll feel a light pulse confirming the action.









![A user holds the bottom third of Apple Pencil Pro between thumb and forefinger, poised to write.](https://www.apple.com/v/apple-pencil/af/images/overview/pro/gallery/haptics_startframe__bdfamr7lfftu_large.jpg)

- ### Apple Pencil hover



Preview exactly where your Apple Pencil will touch down on your display. So you can write, sketch, and illustrate with even greater precision.









![Apple Pencil Pro is pointed downwards towards a display. The display is full of dots which stretch upwards and follow the tip of Apple Pencil Pro, representing how Apple Pencil hover connects Apple Pencil Pro to iPad even before it touches down.](https://www.apple.com/v/apple-pencil/af/images/overview/pro/gallery/hover_startframe__dcxv6n7oem0y_large.jpg)

- ### Double tap



Quickly switch between tools, like a pen and eraser, with a double tap on Apple Pencil Pro.









![A user holds Apple Pencil Pro in a between thumb and forefinger.](https://www.apple.com/v/apple-pencil/af/images/overview/pro/gallery/double_tap_startframe__uv8sldgvszm2_large.jpg)
